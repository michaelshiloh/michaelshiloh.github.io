Machine Art Residency at Hamiffal Gallery

During June and July of 2024 I was given a residency at a wonderful gallery in Jerusalem. Before the residency I started collecting scrap metal and salvaged machinery from around the country. At the gallery I was given a storage room and access to their workshop. As soon as the gallery space opened up, I moved in some of the parts I sorted them out, and started planning machines.
