Residency at Paper Crane Labs

During a one week residency at Paper Crane Labs in Bangalore, India, I collected discarded electronic devices in preparation for a two day workshop in which we would disassemble the devices, salvage reusable electronic components, and build electromechanical contraptions.

During the workshop, we started by learning how to build simple automata from cardboard.
